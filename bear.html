All About Bear

Bear is a beloved pet belonging to the Lamb family.

She is technically a mutt and has characteristics of both Border Collies and German Shepherds.

Likes

Bear is a big fan of:

  • bones
  • toys
  • walks
  • squirrels

Dislikes

Bear dislikes many things, including types of objects and sounds.

Objects

The objects she dislikes fall into two main categories- inanimate and animate objects.

Inanimate Objects

Bear is very thrown off by objects that should not move that inexplicably do, most startlingly when they move in her direction.

Animate Objects

Bear is also not fond of objects that are supposed to move, including Roombas. Once again, the startling effect is amplified when they move in her direction.
